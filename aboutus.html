// Sourdough Bread

About Sourdough Bread

1. It has no store/commercial yeast. 2. Hand kneaded and shaped in-house. 3. Prepared over 14-17hours. 4. Organic flour, the water is filtered and the electricity used to power the oven is solar powered! 5. Store bought bread is mixed and baked within 2 hours meaning the gluten content is really high and can lead to someone feeling clogged up when eating bread. Sourdough is a great alternative and much easier to digest.
